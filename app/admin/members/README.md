# Members Management

Manage mandal members — profiles, contact info, family details, skills, and more.

**Service Entity:** `AdminService.Members` → `Users`

**Main Fields:** full_name, email, phone, city, gender, dob, education, profession, gotra, blood_group

**Object Page Sections:**
- Basic Identity (name, email, phone, profile picture)
- Personal Details (dob, gender, marital status, blood group)
- Address
- Family Details
- Education & Profession
- Religious / Mandal-specific (gotra, nakshatra, rashi, kuldevi)
- Skills & Interests
- Health & Emergency Contact
- Mandal Memberships (table)

**Dev URL:** [http://localhost:4004/admin/members/webapp/index.html](http://localhost:4004/admin/members/webapp/index.html)
